About This Website

Turn name cards into smart, searchable data.

Megapixel Smart Name Card Organizer helps you capture, clean and search your business contacts without digging through piles of cards or random photos on your phone.

  • Upload a card – snap or upload a photo of any name card.
  • Let Azure OCR work – automatically extract name, role, company and more.
  • Review & edit – fix any details before saving to the database.
  • Ask the assistant – in the full version, chat with the bot to find contacts like "Show me all Sales Managers at tech companies."

This prototype showcases how AI and clean UI can work together to make contact management feel modern, simple and actually fun to use.

Why We Built This

We created this website to help individuals and organizations transform physical name cards into meaningful, structured digital information — making it easier to manage contacts, reduce manual data entry, and retrieve the right information at the right time.

By combining intuitive design with Azure-powered OCR and AI assistance, our goal is to help users work more efficiently, stay organized, and focus on building real connections instead of handling paperwork.
